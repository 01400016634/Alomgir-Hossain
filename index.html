About Me

MEL Specialist & Data Analyst

I am a passionate Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL) professional with over 16 years of experience in designing and implementing MEL systems, database management, and reporting across various sectors.

Currently serving as Deputy Coordinator-MEL at Transparency International Bangladesh (TIB), I have previously worked with organizations like Abt Associates, World Vision Bangladesh, and IRRI Bangladesh.

I hold an M.Sc. in Statistics from Rajshahi University and a Post Graduated Diploma (PGD) on MEAL (2025) from ABP, Bangladesh, certified by UK. I have extensive expertise in tools like SPSS, STATA, Python,GIS, Power BI, Kobo Toolbox and NVivo for quantitative and qualitative data analysis and data management.

Digital MEL Hub

Global Learning Platform for MEL Professionals

Digital MEL Hub is a learning platform for solely M&E and research professionals with the vision of connecting all the M&E professional worldwide under one umbrella and build strong sustainable network to enrich professionals' knowledge.

Established on November 20, 2021 through the first webinar, the platform now has nearly 2,300 registered members from more than 120 countries.

The platform serves as a global sharing and learning space to solve MEL issues through webinars, social media groups, and knowledge sharing.
